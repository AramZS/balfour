Satya Ponnuru is a Vice President at Balfour Partners. Mr. Ponnuru brings over 7 years of private equity and investment banking experience. Prior to joining Balfour Partners, Mr. Ponnuru was a member of the investment team at Riverhead Capital, an India focused investment firm, and KPS Capital Partners, a distressed private equity firm. Mr. Ponnuru began his career as an Investment Banker in the Mergers & Acquisitions Group at Deutsche Bank and Broadview International. Mr. Ponnuru holds a Masters of Accounting as well as Bachelor of Science in Finance from the A.B. Freeman School of Business at Tulane University.
