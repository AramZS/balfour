Balfour Investors' fundamental investing principles have allowed its investors over the past three decades to purchase assets significantly below intrinsic value. The opportunity exists today to apply the same principles in small to middle market companies.

The firm's principals have successfully invested in over 75 transactions across a broad range of industries, generating an unlevered 30% IRR for their co-investors throughout a multitude of economic cycles.

Balfour Investors' equity capital is provided by its founders, as well as through a worldwide network of institutional and private investors, some of who have invested with Balfour for over three decades.
