Marc S. Sherman is a Director at Balfour Investors, Inc. Balfour Investors is a leading investment bank specializing in distressed investing for over 35 years. He currently holds an Advisory Board position at Long Ridge Equity Partners, a special opportunities fund. Mr. Sherman is also a Senior Advisor to the P&A Carbon Group LLC, for the U.S. financial and commodity markets. P&A Carbon Group is a carbon project developer and consulting company with offices in Bali, New York, and Milan. Mr. Sherman was a Co-Founder and Chairman of Woodland Trading Corp., a firm specializing in filing positions in publicly listed companies to effect change of control or maximize shareholder value. Prior to Woodland, Mr. Sherman was the founder of The Wilsher Group, dealers and market-makers in the fixed income and commodities markets. The Wilsher Group established an international reputation as innovators in its field. Prior to founding The Wilsher Group, Mr. Sherman was a corporate vice president of Loeb Rhoades & Co and Chairman and Co-Founder of Wilsher Partners, a leading firm in commodity arbitrage. Prior to Loeb Rhoades & Co. Mr. Sherman Co-founded and managed the Financial Services Division of Conti-Commodities Inc., a subsidiary company of the Continental Grain Corp., the largest privately-held commodity operation in the world. Mr. Sherman began his career as an equity research associate and registered representative with the First Manhattan Co. Mr. Sherman is a former member of Comex Inc and the New York Futures Exchange. He has also been a board member of several charitable and civic organizations. Mr. Sherman holds a bachelors degree in Business Administration from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, as well as a Masters Degree in Business Administration, with distinction, from New York University.
