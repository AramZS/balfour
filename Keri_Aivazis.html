Keri Aivazis is a Managing Director at Liber Capital, LLC. Ms. Aivazis brings over a decade of consumer experience and a strong track record helping global beverage companies achieve profitable growth through strategic and operational performance improvement. Her beverage industry experience spans the full range of business issues from corporate strategy, mergers and acquisitions, through to organizational restructuring and supply chain optimization. Most recently, Keri was a founding partner and the Managing Director of the U.S. practice of OC&C Strategy Consultants, a global strategy consultancy focused on consumer oriented industries. Ms. Aivazis holds a Ph. D. in applied mathematics and a M.A. in mechanical engineering from the California Institute of Technology.
